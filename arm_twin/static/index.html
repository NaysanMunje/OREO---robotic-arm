Motor diagnostics

Isolate one motor at a time. Jog tests spin a single motor for ~1.5s and report step count change. If steps change but the shaft doesn't move → driver/power/wiring. If steps stay at 0 → GPIO or firmware issue.

Per-motor tests

Common issues

  • Only one motor moves — check 24V to each driver, PUL+/DIR+ on correct GPIO, and PUL−/DIR− to ESP GND.
  • Limit always pressed — switch may be shorted to GND or wired wrong; homing will fail for that joint.
  • Steps change, shaft still — driver enable (ENA), motor phases, or 24V to that driver only.
  • Jog blocked — homing or move in progress; tap Stop all first.
